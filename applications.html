Applications

Algorithmic Trading

Even though a significant volume of modern-day trading is algorithmic, this volume is not diverse; it is mainly due to a few firms. Most individual and/or small-scale investors still do not have the means to engage in algorithmic trading. Our technology can help mitigate this situation and make algorithmic trading more accessible to all interested investors.

In order to prove our technology, we are currently using it ourselves to engage in algorithmic trading in cryptocurrency exchanges. If successful, this operation will also generate our first revenue stream.

Industrial Internet of Things

The ubiquity of IIoT (Industrial Internet of Things) edge devices has made the Industry 4.0 vision achievable, yet many small/mid-market businesses are not able to take advantage of this promising paradigm shift. We believe that this unfortunate situation is mainly due to the absence of a turnkey end-to-end system that these businesses can adopt with only a small amount of friction to their current day-to-day operations.

We hope to utilize our technology to enable small/mid-market businesses to take advantage of the Industry 4.0 paradigm.
