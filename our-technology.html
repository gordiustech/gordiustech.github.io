Technology

Our technology is "cloud-native": Our software runs on AWS where we leverage Kubernetes to orchestrate our software components, which we develop in Python and containerize via Docker.

We use FoundationDB to store/house time-series data. FoundationDB's CP consistency model and its customizable layers enable us to effectively utilize it as a high-throughput time-series database.

We utilize both in-house ML (Machine Learning) code and also leverage PyTorch for our ML components.
