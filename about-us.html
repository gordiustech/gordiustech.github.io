About Us

Mehmet Ozan Kabak, Ph.D.
Founder

Mehmet Ozan got two B.S. degrees from METU in 2008 (double majoring in Electrical Engineering and Computer Engineering) and M.S./Ph.D. degrees in Electrical Engineering from Stanford University in 2010/2015 where he focused on applications of non-linear optimization techniques. Prior to founding Gordius Technologies, Mehmet Ozan worked in various start-ups in Silicon Valley: Upon graduation, he first joined Striim where he was responsible for adding ML capabilities to Striim's software. After Striim, he joined OptumSoft where he worked closely with the CEO to design, develop and launch OptumSoft's IIoT technology. Lastly, he was with Naver Labs where he worked on core software technologies for autonomous urban air vehicles and helped establish the new Silicon Valley branch.

In his free time, Mehmet Ozan enjoys sailing, playing tennis and spending time with his family.
