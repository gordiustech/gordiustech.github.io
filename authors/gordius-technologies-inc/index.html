Our Company

Mission and Vision

We believe that high-efficiency stream processing, when driven by robust AI techniques, can be a game changing advantage for many businesses. At Gordius Technologies, we strive to develop a complete end-to-end system for processing, analyzing, storing and acting on high-throughput time-series data.

Focus Areas

Our strategy is to introduce our technology into verticals where time-series data is the central "resource" or the "asset" of businesses. As a first step, we chose to focus on the algorithmic trading space. Our second focus area will be the IIoT (Industrial Internet of Things) space.
